Mission

  • To provide a congenial environment for world-class education, research and innovation.
  • To produce highly efficient technical professionals endowed with practical knowledge, skills and ethical values based on emerging demands.
  • To promote multi-faceted academic collaboration across universities and industries for research and innovation.
  • To contribute in national policy making for sustainable socio-economic and industrial development of the country.
  • To provide consultancy in solving technical problems at national and international levels.

History

Dhaka University of Engineering & Technology (DUET), Gazipur was founded as the College of Engineering, Dhaka with the motto “Technology is the Advancement” at its temporary campus at Tejgaon, Dhaka, Bangladesh in 1980. It was then renamed as Dhaka Engineering College (DEC) under the University of Dhaka in 1981, and used to offer Bachelor of Engineering programs in Civil, Electrical, and Mechanical disciplines. With the passage of time, Dhaka Engineering College was empowered autonomy under the Bangladesh Institute of Technology ordinance no. XXI of 1986, under the name Bangladesh Institute of Technology, Dhaka (Abbreviated as BITD) to award undergraduate and postgraduate degrees.
