On 25th June 2022, the Padma Bridge earned the title of the longest bridge in Bangladesh. It is an incredible feat of technical skill built over the flowing Padma River in Bangladesh. This bridge connects the south-western part with the north-eastern part of Bangladesh. This project, the largest in post-independence Bangladesh, has opened the door to new possibilities. The length of the main bridge is 6.15 kilometers and the width is 18.18 meters. This bridge is two-storied; Vehicles will now be on the top and trains will run after the work on the bottom part is completed. The bridge is constructed of concrete and steel. Under the supervision of Bangladesh Army, BUET and Korea Expressway Corporation and Associates, a 12 km link road has been constructed on both sides of the bridge. The Padma Bridge Rail Link Project will involve the construction of 14 new stations and the development and infrastructure of 6 existing stations. Besides, 6 stations of infrastructure development are – Dhaka, Gandaria, Bhanga, Kashiani, Rupdia and Singia. Of the 40 pillars of the main bridge, 40 are in the river and 2 pillars are located on both sides of the river. A total of 240 piles have been made in 6 out of 40 pillars inside the river. Besides, there are 12 piles on both sides of the connecting bridge, totaling 24 piles. The color of Padma Bridge will be golden as golden color absorbs less heat from the sun. The main bridge work was completed by China Major Bridge Engineering Company Limited. New momentum will come in business. The dream of Bengalis with their own money is now on the way to reality after overcoming various uncertainties. Everyone hopes that this bridge will soon change the country’s economy and improve people’s living conditions. Initially in 2007 the cost of Padma Bridge was estimated at 10 thousand 161 crore 75 lakh taka. On its eastern bank is Lauhjung of Munshiganj and on its western bank Shariatpur and Madaripur. The total length of this bridge is 6,150 meters and width is 18.18 meters. The construction of this bridge started on December 7, 2014 with only concrete and steel. It was built by China Major Bridge Engineering Construction Company Limited. 20,000 people including 2,000 foreigners are directly involved in the various works of the project. Through the opening of this bridge, 19 districts of the southwestern region have been directly connected with the eastern part of the country, including Dhaka, and most of the districts will be connected when the railway section is opened.
